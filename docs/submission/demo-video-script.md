# Demo Video Script

**Target length:** 2:30–3:00 (confirm against actual portal limit before final cut)
**Format:** actual product footage + real or clearly labeled role-play call audio, captions burned in
**Editor/director:** Arsalan · **Footage source:** Members 1–3 (see asset-manifest.md)

Every line below is a draft. Nothing gets recorded as narration or on-screen text until the matching row in `claim-ledger.md` is VERIFIED.

---

**0:00–0:15 — Problem**
- On-screen text: "The same move. Five times the price difference. Hours of phone calls."
- Voiceover / title card: "VeraMove calls, compares, and negotiates for you."
- Visual: split-screen of two wildly different quote totals for the "same" move.

**0:15–0:40 — Intake**
- Show one path live: ElevenLabs voice interview *or* document upload (pick whichever is more reliable at record time).
- Caption: "Voice or document — same structured job spec either way."
- Cut briefly to the other path's output to show the JobSpec matches. *(Requires: JobSpecV1 claim = VERIFIED)*

**0:40–0:55 — Confirmation**
- Show the confirmation screen: move date, addresses, stairs, elevator, inventory, packing needs, special items.
- User clicks Confirm and Start Calls.
- Caption: "Locked once. Reused for every call."

**0:55–1:35 — Three calls (≈7–10s clips each)**
- Vendor A (transparent): itemized quote appears in structured form.
- Vendor B (cheap-then-hidden-fees): fee surfaces only under questioning — this is the hidden-fee beat.
- Vendor C (premium): higher quote, sets up the negotiation payoff.
- Caption per clip: name the vendor style on screen ("Transparent," "Hidden fee found," "Premium").
- If any vendor is role-play: small persistent caption "Simulated vendor — role-played by team" for the duration of that clip.

**1:35–2:05 — Negotiation**
- Show the callback to the strongest vendor with the verified competing quote.
- Play the audio where price or terms change.
- On-screen: before → after numbers, exact figures, no rounding language.
- Caption: "Verified quote used as leverage — never invented." *(Requires: leverage claim = VERIFIED)*

**2:05–2:35 — Recommendation**
- Dashboard: ranked vendors, all-in totals, hidden-fee warnings, binding status, transcript excerpts.
- Click through to one piece of transcript evidence to prove the link is real, not decorative.
- Caption: "Every conclusion links back to the call."

**2:35–2:50 — Positioning**
- On-screen line: "VeraMove operates independently today and is designed to become a move-in concierge for VeraAI as the broader housing platform matures."
